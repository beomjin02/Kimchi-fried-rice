Kimchi Fried Rice


It's a kind of fried rice. Put some kimchi, vegetable and rice. And then, fry them together. Finally, enjoy the kimchi fried rice.

Ingredients

egg / oil / kimchi / leek / onion / ham

How to make the kimchi fried rice?

1. Put some oil in the pan.
2. Fry an egg.
3. When the egg is cooked, cut the egg.
4. Put some kimchi, onion, ham and leek in the pan.
5. When the onion is cooked, put some rice in the pan.
